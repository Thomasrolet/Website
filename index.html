Our Story

Motoldcycle came to me from a childhood idea, I remember when I was 12 my father made me a bike with his own hands. The latter was very dear to me emotionally, but above all it was unique and had a real story. Of all my friends I was the only one to perceive this precise feeling when I climbed on my bike. Today, our goal is to create your personalized motorcycle, the one you will cherish, that will make you feel unique.
